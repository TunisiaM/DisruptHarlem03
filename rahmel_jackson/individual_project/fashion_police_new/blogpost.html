Fashion Police

by Rahmel jackson

Remember how you dressed when you were a kid? All Those graphic hoodies embedded with images of cats, shirts filled with various band names, jeans too baggy or too loose, and how we watched slowly throughout the years,we moved on into to different styles, some eveyr year, other and hold on forever, However, what is the the mechanisms behind these movements?

"The Symmetrical and asymmetrical nature of Familiarity and Identification"

Familiarity is our historical perceptive to things that are similar to what we've seen before, this can be concepts, physical structures or emotions. Recent studies shows for good or bad, we are attracted to what's Familiar; it could be that it lowers anxiety because we know what to expect, we could manage our fear with our predefined reactions to things that cause us pain, or it's simply, higher enjoyable and we seek to find it everywhere. However, The familiar plays a deeper role on personal level, for when we begin to look into ourselves, we start to find things about us we like and seek to promote, and don't like and we scorn and repress, this idealizing and devaluing of ourselves, goes beyond just oneself and the process of projection on situations, beings, and even objects we encounter, and form our actions we premeditate the world with thoughts of good and bad.
