"Putting The Fashion World On Lock"

Fashion police was developed with the understanding in a world of increasing speed of innovations either ideologically or technologically, people need to be aware of the byproducts of their consumption, as well the ideas they subscribe to, form to the rigorous demands on the workers of various industries that provide us the goods and services daily, to the way these rapid changes affect relationships, health, and overall well being.


Fashion is one of the most personal and political thing about ourselves, however, it's covered very shallowly. Most fashion sites look for daily trends, celebrity news, and press releases. Not only does this make for subpar writing content, but because of their reactive nature, their content tends to be the same as everyone else's.
